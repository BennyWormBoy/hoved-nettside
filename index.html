プロジェクトセカイ カラフルステージ！ feat. 初音ミク
Project Sekai, HATSUNE MIKU: COLORFUL STAGE!

Project Sekai is a Japanese mobile rythm game by colorful Palette and published by SEGA in 2020.
The game is a spin-off of SEGA's Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA series. Project Sekai features 6 well known vocaloids,
Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin, Kagamine Len, Megurine Luka, MEIKO and KAITO, as well as 20 pjsk original characters.
The game is not only a rythm game but it also has a gatcha system and have quite an amout of stories.

ポケットモンスター ベストウイッシュ(Pocket Monsters: Best Wishes!)
Pokémon Black and White

Pokémon Black version and White version are generation 5 of the Pokémon video game series.
The RPG is developed by Game Freak and published by The Pokémon Company and Nintendo for the Nintendo DS in 2010.
Pokémon Black and White introduced 156 new Pokémon, including generation 5's starter Pokémon
Snivy, Tepig and Oshawott, bringing the total from 493 to 649.

Favorite games I havent played but watched:

ゴースト トリック
Ghost Trick: Phantom Detective

Ghost Trick is a puzzle adventure story game developed and published by Capcom in 2010.
The story follows Sissle a ghost on a journey to rediscover his identity withe the help of his ghost tricks(powers).
This beautiful work of art was not only directed by the creator of the Ace Attorney game series, Shu Takumi,
but the music was also composed by Yasumasa Kitagawa, known for composing for Ace Attorney.

Home Saftey Hotline

Home Safety Hotline is an analog horror telephone operator simulator set in 1996, developed and published by
Night Signal Entertainment in 2024.
In the game you play as a safetey hotline oporator who respond to calls and providing the callers with information on
ordinary and supernatural household hazards.

Little Nightmares ( 1 and 2 + DLCs)

Little Nightmares is a third person horror puzzle-platform adventure game, where your survival is your top priority,
developed by Tarsier Studios and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment.

Little Nightmares, originally supposed to be called "Hunger", follows the journey of Six, a hungry little girl seen wearing a yellow raincoat
who wandered onto The Maw, an iron floating vessle in the water, looking to ease the hunger. upon entering we see what seems to be big fat
monstrous, twisted beings, or "adults" as well as cute little knomes who help you


Little nightmares 2, the prequel to little nightmares, follows Mono, a boy with supernatural powers, who seems to be wearing a
paper bag over his head. as you start to explore you meet Six, the protagonist of the previous game.
You work together and venture through the Pale City to stop the Signal Tower.

Secrets of The Maw is downloadable content for game divided into three chapters which were released separetly: Depth, The Hideaway and The Residence.
In the DLC you play as the Runaway Kid trying to escape. Through the story following the Runaway Kid the player gets an opportunity to learn more about The Maw.
