プロジェクトセカイ カラフルステージ！ feat. 初音ミク
(Project Sekai, HATSUNE MIKU: COLORFUL STAGE!)

Project Sekai is a Japanese mobile rythm game by colorful Palette and published by SEGA in 2020.
The game is a spin-off of SEGA's Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA series. Project Sekai features 6 well known vocaloids,
Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin, Kagamine Len, Megurine Luka, MEIKO and KAITO, as well as 20 pjsk original charachters.
The game is not only a rythm game but it also has a gatcha system and have quite an amout of stories.

Pokémon Black and White

Pokémon Black version and White version are generation 5 of the Pokémon video game series.
The RPG is developed by Game Freak and published by The Pokémon Company and Nintendo for the Nintendo DS in 2010.
Pokémon Black and White introduced 156 new Pokémon, including generation 5's starter Pokémon
Snivy, Tepig and Oshawott, bringing the total from 493 to 649.

Favorite games I havent played but watched:

ゴースト トリック
(Ghost Trick: Phantom Detective)

Ghost Trick is a puzzle adventure story game developed and published by Capcom in 2010.
The story follows Sissle a ghost on a journey to rediscover his identity withe the help of his ghost tricks(powers).
This beautiful work of art was not only directed by the creator of the Ace Attorney game series, Shu Takumi,
but the music was also composed by Yasumasa Kitagawa, known for composing for Ace Attorney.
